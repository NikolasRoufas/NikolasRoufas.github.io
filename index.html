Publications

Conference Paper

Analyzing Public Discourse and Sentiment in Climate Change Discussions Using Transformer-Based Models

N.Roufas , A. Mohasseb, I. Karamitsos & A. Kanavos.

Artificial Intelligence Applications & Innovations (AIAI), 2025

This paper analyzes sentiment trends in climate change discussions on social media using transformer-based models. By fine-tuning ClimateBERT on Reddit data (2013–2024), it examines misinformation patterns and ideological polarization. Results show that deep learning outperforms traditional methods, achieving a 90% F1-score. The study highlights challenges like sarcasm and bias, offering insights for policymakers and media analysts.
